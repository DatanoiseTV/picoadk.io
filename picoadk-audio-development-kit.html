PicoADK - Audio Development Kit

The PicoADK series is a platform of embedded microcontroller boards, perfect for building your own standalone sound toys, synthesizers, noise machines, and more. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, PicoADK has the tools you need to bring your creative audio projects to life

PicoADK v1 – Your Entry to Embedded Sound Design

Designed for simple yet powerful projects, the PicoADK v1 is the perfect entry-level model. Powered by the Raspberry Pi RP2040 Dual Core Cortex M0+ MCU, it excels at handling audio tasks with fixed-point math, making it ideal for lightweight synthesizers or sound experiments.

PicoADK v2 – Advanced Features for Complex Creations

The PicoADK v2 takes your projects to the next level, featuring the powerful Raspberry Pi RP2350 MCU with an impressive setup: 2 RISC-V Hazard 3 cores and 2 ARM Cortex-M33 cores, all clocked at 150 MHz. This model supports overclocking for even more performance, making it ideal for complex audio synthesis, effects processing, and beyond.

For memory-intensive projects, the PicoADK v2 also offers an 8MB external PSRAM option and includes a Micro TF-Card slot for additional storage. With these enhancements, the PicoADK v2 is the ultimate choice for creators looking to push the boundaries of embedded audio technology.
